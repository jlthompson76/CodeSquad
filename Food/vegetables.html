What are Vegetables?

Vegetables are part of plants that are consumed by humans as food as part of a meal.

History of Vegetables

Orignially, vegetables were collected from the wild by hunters and gatherers and entered cultivation in several part of the world, probably during the period 10,000 BC to 7,000 BC.
